Why Leave Home? Push & Pull Factors

PUSH

Reasons to LEAVE a place.

  • No jobs, poverty
  • Conflict, war, bad governance
  • Natural disasters, persecution

PULL

Reasons to GO to a place.

  • Job opportunities, better salary
  • Education, safety, family
  • Political freedom, quality of life
